FAQ

    • What are the standard shipping times?

      Normally, the blender can be shipped within 2 to 4 business days.

    • How to care for Sleek Soul?

      • Although impact-resistant materials are used to manufacture Sleek Soul blenders, handling them requires care.
      • A handle on the bottle cap allows you to carry it around all day. However, Sleek Soul must be protected from direct sunlight and should not be left in a hot car in summer since high temperatures can cause rubber seals inside and outside the blender to perish.
      • When not using it, remove the lid from your Sleek Soul. Such air-drying will prevent the buildup of moisture in the blender itself, as well as around its seal. In this way, Sleek Soul will preserve fresh smell and look for a long time.
      • Avoid adding fizzy liquids like sparkling water or soft drinks to the blend, as they can create too high pressure in the bottle, resulting in its breakage.
      • Never pour boiling water into the Sleek Soul blender! The maximum allowed water temperature should not exceed 70 degrees.
      • Although all the blender parts (excluding the motor) are dishwasher-safe, we recommend washing it by hand in warm water with dish detergent. Before doing so, twist your Sleek Soul apart completely.

    • Is a warranty given?

      Yes, a two-year warranty is provided to consumers, so the purchase receipt needs to be kept.

    • Is Sleek Soul intended for blending frozen fruits?

      Yes, Sleek Soul is intended for blending frozen fruits. Please bear in mind that even a fully charged battery can make fewer blends in this case due to their hardness.

    • How much time is needed
      to recharge the battery?

      The Sleek Soul battery will be fully charged after 2 hours, thanks to a supplied wireless charger.

    • What is the number of blends the full battery allows doing?

      When the battery is full, it can blend as many as 12 times before it will need to be recharged.
      Everything depends on the ingredients you are going to blend.
      Making a protein shake requires less power than mixing fresh produce, for example.

    • Can the Sleek Soul bottle
      be stored in a fridge?

      Yes, the door of your fridge will be a good storage place for Sleek Soul.
      Avoid placing the bottle toward the back of your fridge.

    • Is it loud?

      Mix Juicer is ultra-quiet and makes no buzzing sound like traditional blenders. Quiet Enough For A Library!
